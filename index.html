The only true 2048 game.. featuring our Queen Kim Yura.. <3

Combine Yuras to get ALL DA YURAS!!

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the Yuras. When two tiles with the same Yura match, they merge into another Yura! You win by reaching the last Yura tile. See how far you can get!
